Privacy Policy

How BoyoPrep collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: January 2026

📋 Overview

Welcome to BoyoPrep ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at boyoprep.com (or our GitHub Pages address).

By using BoyoPrep, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully.

📥 Information We Collect

BoyoPrep does not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use the platform. However, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Referring website (how you found BoyoPrep)
  • Quiz interactions (subject selected, questions answered)
We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

📊 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use BoyoPrep. This helps us improve the platform and serve students better.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages you visit on BoyoPrep
  • How long you spend on the site
  • Your approximate location (country/city)
  • Device and browser information

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

💰 Google AdSense & Advertising

BoyoPrep uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. These ads help us keep the platform free for all students.

Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show you relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Limit how often you see the same ad
Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visits to BoyoPrep and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

🍪 Cookies

BoyoPrep uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies — needed for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to track usage
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of BoyoPrep.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve and optimise BoyoPrep's content and user experience
  • Understand which subjects and features students use most
  • Display relevant advertisements to keep the platform free
  • Monitor and fix technical issues on the website

🔗 Third-Party Services

BoyoPrep uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts served from Google's servers

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

👨‍🎓 Students & Minors

BoyoPrep is designed for secondary school and university students preparing for JAMB and WAEC. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since no registration is required, students can use BoyoPrep safely without providing any personal details.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of BoyoPrep after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
