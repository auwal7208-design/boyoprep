About BoyoPrep

Built for Students,
By a Student Champion

BoyoPrep is a free Computer-Based Test practice platform dedicated to helping Nigerian students pass JAMB and WAEC with confidence.

Our Mission

Every year, thousands of Nigerian students sit for JAMB and WAEC — and many of them don't pass, not because they aren't smart, but because they didn't have access to the right practice tools.

BoyoPrep was created to change that. We believe every student — regardless of location or background — deserves access to quality CBT practice that mirrors the real exam experience. Our platform gives students unlimited access to past questions, instant results, and detailed answer reviews, completely free.

Our goal is simple: help every Nigerian student that uses BoyoPrep pass their exams.
